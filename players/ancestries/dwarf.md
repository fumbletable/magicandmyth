# Dwarf

*Strong and hearty mountain-dwellers, foremost authorities of mining and engineering.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Attribute Adjustments** | CON +2, CHA -1 |
| **Speed** | 25 ft |
| **Size** | Medium |
| **Languages** | Common, Dwarven |
| **Bonus Languages** | Gnome, Giant, Goblin, Kobold, Orc |
| **Senses** | Darkvision 60 ft |

## Ancestral Traits

**Protection from Magic**
: +2 bonus to Fortitude saving throw, +1 bonus to Will saving throw.

**Magic Disruption**
: Natural defense against magic items (not weapons, armor, shields, gauntlets, girdles). Roll d%: 1-80 works, 81-100 disrupted. Cannot learn arcane spellcasting unless ancestral trait is suppressed. Divine spells are unaffected.

**Ancestral Enemies**
: +1 to attack rolls against orcs, half-orcs, goblins, and hobgoblins.

**Giant Guard**
: +4 temporary AC bonus against all attacks from Large or bigger creatures.

**Stonecunning**
: Can discern features of slope or worked stone within 20 feet with a Wisdom/Perception check.

## Proficiencies

- Minor proficiency in either Engineering or Mining.
- Proficiency in either hammers or picks (one and two-handed versions).

## Weapons & Armor

Any allowed by class. Dwarves tend not to use bows or elven armor.

## Allowed Classes

Barbarian, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Paladin, Ranger, Thief.

{: .warning }
> **Wizard Restriction:** Dwarves cannot learn arcane spellcasting due to Magic Disruption, unless the trait is suppressed.
