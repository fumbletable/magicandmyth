# Half-Orc

*Born from the union of orc and human, hardened survivalists with a frightening temper.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Attribute Adjustments** | STR +1, CON +1, CHA -1 |
| **Speed** | 30 ft |
| **Size** | Medium |
| **Languages** | Common, Orc |
| **Bonus Languages** | Giant, Gnoll, Kobold, Goblin |
| **Senses** | Darkvision 60 ft, Low-Light Vision (human daylight range) |

## Ancestral Traits

**Frenzy**
: Once per day, lasting up to 10 rounds. While in frenzy: -2 AC, +2 attack and damage. You fight until -10 HP (then die immediately). Enter as a secondary action, exiting early costs a full round.

## Proficiencies

- Minor proficiency in either Wilderness Survival or Mountaineering.
- Proficient in any one axe and unarmed attacks.

## Weapons & Armor

Any allowed by class.

## Allowed Classes

Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Thief, Wizard.

{: .note }
> **Paladin Note:** Gnomes and half-orcs often fail to qualify. Only lawful half-orcs may petition.
