# Halfling

*Peaceful, cheerful small folk who are supremely lucky.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Attribute Adjustments** | STR -2, DEX +2, CHA +1 |
| **Speed** | 25 ft |
| **Size** | Small |
| **Languages** | Common, Halfling |
| **Bonus Languages** | Dwarven, Elven, Gnome, Giant, Kobold, Goblin |
| **Senses** | Low-Light Vision (twice human range) |

## Ancestral Traits

**Protection from Magic**
: +2 bonus to Will saving throw.

**Poison Resistance**
: +2 bonus on Fortitude saves against natural poisons.

**Uncanny Luck**
: One re-roll per day per four levels of experience. Can re-roll your own roll or force the GM to re-roll.

## Proficiencies

- Free proficiency in slings and one of: javelins, throwing dagger, throwing axe, or spear.
- +1 attack and damage with slings.

## Weapons & Armor

Any allowed by class. Weapons are one size smaller, dealing one less damage die category.

## Allowed Classes

Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Ranger, Thief, Wizard.
