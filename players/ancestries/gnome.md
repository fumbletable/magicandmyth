# Gnome

*Enterprising small humanoids, amazing engineers and born illusionists.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Attribute Adjustments** | STR -2, INT +1, CHA +2 |
| **Speed** | 25 ft |
| **Size** | Small |
| **Languages** | Common, Gnome |
| **Bonus Languages** | Dwarf, Halfling, Kobold, Goblin |
| **Senses** | Low-Light Vision (twice human range) |

## Ancestral Traits

**Protection from Magic**
: +1 bonus to all saving throws.

**Magic Disruption**
: Similar to dwarves but does not disrupt illusion items or items made for gnomes/gnome's class. Cannot cast arcane spells except illusions. Divine spells are unaffected.

**Ancestral Enemies**
: +1 bonus to attack rolls against kobolds and goblins.

**Defensive Training**
: +4 temporary AC bonus when fighting gnolls, bugbears, trolls, ogres, and giants.

**Underground Survival**
: Can discern features of natural passages/caverns within 20 feet with a Wisdom/Perception check.

**Illusions**
: Once per day, may cast one of: ventriloquism, minor cantrip (temporary effect), phantasmal image (no larger than Tiny). Caster level equals character level.

## Proficiencies

- Minor proficiency in Gemcutting and Engineering.
- Proficient with shortspear. Double damage when set for charge.

## Weapons & Armor

Any allowed by class. Weapons are one size smaller, dealing one less damage die category (e.g., 1d6 becomes 1d4).

## Allowed Classes

Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Thief.

{: .warning }
> **Wizard Restriction:** Gnomes may only be specialist illusionists. Only gnomes may multi-class with illusionist.
