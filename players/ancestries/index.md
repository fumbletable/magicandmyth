# Ancestries

Your ancestry is where you come from — and what you can do that others can't. Choose one of seven ancestries for your character.

Every character speaks **Common** plus any languages granted by their ancestry. If your Intelligence modifier is positive, you gain that many bonus languages from your ancestry's list.

| Ancestry | Attribute Adjustments | Speed | Key Traits |
|----------|----------------------|-------|------------|
| [Dwarf](dwarf) | CON +2, CHA -1 | 25 ft | Darkvision, Magic Protection, Stonecunning |
| [Elf](elf) | DEX +1, WIS +1, CON -1 | 35 ft | Low-Light Vision, Danger Sense, Sleep Immunity |
| [Gnome](gnome) | STR -2, INT +1, CHA +2 | 25 ft | Low-Light Vision, Innate Illusions, Small |
| [Half-Elf](half-elf) | DEX +1 | 30 ft | Low-Light Vision, Bonus Proficiency |
| [Half-Orc](half-orc) | STR +1, CON +1, CHA -1 | 30 ft | Darkvision, Frenzy |
| [Halfling](halfling) | STR -2, DEX +2, CHA +1 | 25 ft | Low-Light Vision, Uncanny Luck, Small |
| [Human](human) | None | 30 ft | Most versatile — all classes available |
