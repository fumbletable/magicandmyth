# Half-Elf

*Offspring of elves and humans, ambitious and eager to belong.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Attribute Adjustments** | DEX +1 |
| **Speed** | 30 ft |
| **Size** | Medium |
| **Languages** | Common (Elven if raised in elven culture) |
| **Bonus Languages** | Elven, Dwarven, Gnome, Goblin, Halfling, Hobgoblin, Orc |
| **Senses** | Low-Light Vision 60 ft |

## Ancestral Traits

**Enhanced Focus**
: +1 bonus on checks to notice secret or concealed objects.

**Elven Strength of Mind**
: 50% magic resistance against magical sleep effects. +1 Will saving throw vs mind-affecting spells (illusions, charms). Stacks with Wisdom bonus.

**Ambitious Start**
: One additional proficiency slot at character creation (nonweapon or weapon).

## Proficiencies

- Free proficiency in longsword, elven thin blade, rapier, or any bow.

## Weapons & Armor

Any not forbidden by class.

## Allowed Classes

Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Druid, Fighter, Monk, Paladin, Ranger, Thief, Wizard.
