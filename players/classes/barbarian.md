# Barbarian

*Fierce warriors from the wild lands, driven by instinct and fury.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d12 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | Human, Half-Orc, Half-Elf |
| **Alignment** | Any non-lawful |
| **Armor** | Any (some abilities restricted in heavy armor) |
| **Weapon Groups** | All |

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| STR | 12 | 16 |
| CON | 12 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimums: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Human or Half-Orc): +10% XP.

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Starting Slots** | 4 | 3 |
| **New Slot Every** | 3rd level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -2 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | General, Nature |

| Fortitude | Reflex | Will |
|-----------|--------|------|
| 5 | 5 | 4 |
