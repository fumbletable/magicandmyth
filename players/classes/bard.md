# Bard

*Wandering performers, scholars, and jacks-of-all-trades who weave magic through music and lore.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d6 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | Human, Half-Elf, Halfling, Gnome |
| **Alignment** | Any neutral (must have neutrality on one axis) |
| **Armor** | Leather, padded, studded leather, brigandine, elven/mithral chain. No shields. |
| **Weapon Groups** | All (any weapon no larger than the bard) |

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------------|
| INT | 16 |
| CHA | 16 |

Meeting prime example (Human, Half-Elf, Halfling, or Gnome): +10% XP.

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Starting Slots** | 2 | 3 |
| **New Slot Every** | 4th level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -3 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | Academic, General, Rogue |

|-----------|--------|------|
