# Cleric

*Holy warriors and healers, empowered by the gods they serve.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | All |
| **Alignment** | Within one step of deity's alignment |
| **Armor** | Any. Shields allowed. Weapon restrictions vary by mythos. |
| **Weapon Groups** | Clubs/Maces, Flails, Picks/Hammers, Simple Melee |

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| WIS | 10 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimum: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Human or Dwarf): +10% XP. Specific mythoi may require additional attributes.

## Spellcasting

- **Type:** Divine
- **Attribute:** Wisdom
- **Access:** Spheres determined by mythos

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Starting Slots** | 2 | 3 |
| **New Slot Every** | 4th level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -3 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | Academic, General |

## Saving Throws (Level 1)

| Fortitude | Reflex | Will |
|-----------|--------|------|
| 5 | 4 | 6 |
