# Thief

*Cunning rogues who rely on skill, stealth, and a well-placed blade.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d6 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | All |
| **Alignment** | Any non-lawful |
| **Armor** | Leather, padded, studded leather, brigandine, elven/mithral chain. No shields. |
| **Weapon Groups** | Crossbows, Finesse, Simple Melee, Simple Ranged, Whips |

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| DEX | 14 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimum: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Halfling, Half-Elf, or Human): +10% XP.
