# Monk

*Disciplined martial artists who channel inner focus into devastating combat prowess.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | Human, Half-Elf, Half-Orc |
| **Alignment** | Any lawful |
| **Armor** | None. AC improves with level. |
| **Weapon Groups** | Martial Arts |

Monks may use any weapon no larger than themselves, with exceptions for ranged or martial arts weapons.

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| STR | 14 | 14 |
| DEX | 14 | 14 |
| CON | 14 | 14 |
| WIS | 14 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimums: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Human, Half-Elf, or Half-Orc): +10% XP.

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Starting Slots** | 2 | 3 |
| **New Slot Every** | 4th level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -3 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | Academic, General |
