# Classes

Your class defines your role in the party — how you fight, what abilities you gain, and how you advance. Some classes have attribute requirements; failing to meet them costs -10% XP. Meeting the "prime example" requirements grants +10% XP.

| Class | Hit Die | Allowed Ancestries | Alignment | Key Feature |
|-------|---------|-------------------|-----------|-------------|
| [Barbarian](barbarian) | d12 | Human, Half-Orc, Half-Elf | Any non-lawful | Rage, Fast Movement |
| [Bard](bard) | d6 | Human, Half-Elf, Halfling, Gnome | Any neutral | Bardic music, Versatile |
| [Cleric](cleric) | d8 | All | Within 1 step of deity | Divine spells, Turn Undead |
| [Druid](druid) | d8 | All | True Neutral | Nature magic, Shapechange |
| [Fighter](fighter) | d10 | All | Any | Weapon Specialization |
| [Monk](monk) | d8 | Human, Half-Elf, Half-Orc | Any lawful | Unarmed combat, AC progression |
| [Paladin](paladin) | d10 | All | Lawful Good | Lay on Hands, Divine spells |
| [Ranger](ranger) | d10 | Human, Half-Elf, Elf | Any good | Tracking, Two-weapon fighting |
| [Thief](thief) | d6 | All | Any non-lawful | Sneak Attack, Rogue Skills |
| [Wizard](wizard) | d4 | Human, Half-Elf, Elf, Half-Orc, Halfling | Any | Arcane spells, Specialization |
