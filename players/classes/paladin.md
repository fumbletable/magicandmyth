# Paladin

*Holy knights sworn to uphold law and goodness, blessed with divine power.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | All |
| **Alignment** | Lawful Good |
| **Armor** | Any. Shields allowed. |
| **Weapon Groups** | All |

{: .note }
> Gnomes and half-orcs often fail to qualify due to chaotic/evil tendencies. Only lawful half-orcs may petition.

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| STR | 12 | 16 |
| CON | 10 | 10 |
| WIS | 13 | 13 |
| CHA | 15 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimums: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Human): +10% XP.

## Spellcasting

- **Type:** Divine
- **Attribute:** Wisdom
- **Access:** Limited divine spells at higher levels

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|

| **New Slot Every** | 3rd level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -2 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | Academic, General |
