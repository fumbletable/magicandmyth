# Wizard

*Scholars of the arcane arts, bending reality through study and willpower.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d4 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | Human, Half-Elf, Elf, Half-Orc, Halfling |
| **Alignment** | Any |
| **Armor** | None (cannot cast somatic spells while armored, unless Light Armor Use talent) |
| **Weapons Allowed** | Dagger, dart, knife, sling, staff |

{: .note }
> Dwarves and gnomes cannot be wizards due to Magic Disruption. Exception: gnomes may be specialist illusionists.

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| INT | 14 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimum: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Half-Elf or Human): +10% XP. Gnome illusionists with INT 16+ also qualify.

## Spellcasting

- **Type:** Arcane
- **Attribute:** Intelligence
- **Access:** All schools (generalist) or restricted by specialization
