# Druid

*Guardians of the natural world, wielding the power of earth, wind, and wild things.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d8 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | All |
| **Alignment** | True Neutral |
| **Armor** | Leather, padded, hide. Wooden shields only. |
| **Weapons Allowed** | Club, dagger, dart, scimitar, sickle, sling, spear, staff |

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| WIS | 15 | 17 |

Failing to meet minimum: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Human or Half-Elf): +10% XP.

## Spellcasting

- **Type:** Divine
- **Attribute:** Wisdom
- **Access:** Nature-focused spheres

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Starting Slots** | 2 | 3 |
| **New Slot Every** | 4th level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -3 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | Academic, General, Nature |

| Fortitude | Reflex | Will |
|-----------|--------|------|
| 5 | 4 | 6 |
