# Ranger

*Skilled trackers and wilderness warriors, equally deadly with bow and blade.*

## At a Glance

| | |
|---|---|
| **Hit Die** | d10 |
| **Allowed Ancestries** | Human, Half-Elf, Elf |
| **Alignment** | Any good (Lawful Good, Neutral Good, Chaotic Good) |
| **Armor** | Any (some abilities penalized in heavy armor) |
| **Weapon Groups** | All |

## Attribute Requirements

| Attribute | Minimum | Prime Example |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| STR | 13 | 16 |
| DEX | 13 | 16 |
| CON | 14 | — |
| WIS | 14 | 16 |

Failing to meet minimums: -10% XP. Meeting prime example (Elf or Half-Elf): +10% XP.

## Spellcasting

- **Type:** Divine
- **Attribute:** Wisdom
- **Access:** Limited divine spells at higher levels

## Proficiency Slots

| | Weapon | Nonweapon |
|---|--------|-----------|
| **Starting Slots** | 4 | 3 |
| **New Slot Every** | 3rd level | — |
| **Non-proficiency Penalty** | -2 | — |
| **NWP Groups** | — | General, Nature |

## Saving Throws (Level 1)

| Fortitude | Reflex | Will |
|-----------|--------|------|
| 5 | 5 | 5 |
