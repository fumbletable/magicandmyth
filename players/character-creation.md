# Character Creation

Follow these steps to create a Magic&Myth character.

## Step 1: Roll Attributes

Roll your six attributes: **Strength**, **Dexterity**, **Constitution**, **Intelligence**, **Wisdom**, and **Charisma**.

| Method | How It Works |
|--------|-------------|
| **Standard** | Roll 4d6, drop lowest. Do this six times, assign in order. |
| **Classic** | Roll 3d6 six times, assign in order. |
| **Heroic** | Roll 4d6, drop lowest, six times. Assign to any attribute. |
| **Loaded** | Roll 3d6, re-roll any 1s. Six times, assign to any attribute. |

See [Attributes](attributes) for full modifier tables.

## Step 2: Choose an Ancestry

Your ancestry determines your starting traits, attribute adjustments, senses, and which classes are available to you.

Available ancestries: [Dwarf](ancestries/dwarf), [Elf](ancestries/elf), [Gnome](ancestries/gnome), [Half-Elf](ancestries/half-elf), [Half-Orc](ancestries/half-orc), [Halfling](ancestries/halfling), [Human](ancestries/human).

## Step 3: Choose a Class

Your class determines your hit die, combat ability, special features, and progression. Some classes have attribute requirements and ancestry restrictions.

Available classes: [Barbarian](classes/barbarian), [Bard](classes/bard), [Cleric](classes/cleric), [Druid](classes/druid), [Fighter](classes/fighter), [Monk](classes/monk), [Paladin](classes/paladin), [Ranger](classes/ranger), [Thief](classes/thief), [Wizard](classes/wizard).

## Step 4: Note Saving Throws

Each class has base saving throws at level 1 across three categories:
- **Fortitude** — resisting poison, disease, physical effects
- **Reflex** — dodging area effects, traps
- **Will** — resisting mental effects, magic

## Step 5: Choose Proficiencies

You get weapon proficiency slots and nonweapon proficiency slots based on your class. See [Proficiencies](proficiencies/) for details.

## Step 6: Buy Equipment

Roll starting gold based on your class, then purchase weapons, armor, and gear.

| Class | Starting Gold |
|-------|--------------|
| Fighter | 5d4 x 10 gp |
| Paladin | 5d4 x 10 gp |
| Ranger | 5d4 x 10 gp |
| Barbarian | 3d6 x 10 gp |
| Cleric | 3d6 x 10 gp |
| Druid | 3d6 x 10 gp |
| Bard | 2d6 x 10 gp |
| Thief | 2d6 x 10 gp |
| Wizard | (1d4+1) x 10 gp |
| Monk | 1d4 x 10 gp |

See [Equipment](equipment/) for full item lists and prices.

## Step 7: Calculate Derived Stats

- **Hit Points:** Roll your class hit die (max at level 1), add CON modifier.
- **Armor Class:** Base 10 + armor AC bonus + DEX defense modifier + shield bonus.
- **Initiative Die:** Determined by your weapon (see weapon tables).
- **Base To-Hit (BTH):** From your class level chart.

## Step 8: Fill In the Details

Choose a name, alignment, and background. You're ready to play.
