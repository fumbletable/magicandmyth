# Combat

## The Combat Round

Each round represents roughly 6 seconds of in-game time. A round follows this sequence:

1. **Declare actions** — Each side states what they intend to do.
2. **Roll initiative** — Each combatant rolls their initiative die (determined by weapon).
3. **Resolve actions** — Actions resolve from lowest initiative roll to highest (lower is faster).

## Initiative

Your initiative die is determined by the weapon you're using (shown in the weapon tables). Lighter weapons have smaller dice (faster). Unarmed attacks use d4.

**Modifiers:**
- Elves reduce their initiative die by one step (d12 becomes d10, etc.)
- Large shields add +1 to initiative
- Tower shields add +2 to initiative

## Making an Attack

**Roll d20 + Base To-Hit (BTH) + Attribute Modifier**

- Melee attacks use STR modifier
- Ranged attacks use DEX modifier
- Compare the total against the target's **Armor Class (AC)**
- Meet or exceed AC = hit

## Armor Class

**AC = 10 + Armor Bonus + Shield Bonus + DEX Defense Modifier**

An unarmored character with 10 DEX has AC 10.

## Damage

On a hit, roll your weapon's damage die + STR modifier (melee only).

**Damage vs Large creatures:** Some weapons have different damage against Large or bigger targets (see weapon tables).

## Saving Throws

There are three saving throw categories:

| Save | Used Against | Key Attribute |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Fortitude** | Poison, disease, physical effects | Constitution |
| **Reflex** | Area effects, traps, dodging | Dexterity |
| **Will** | Mental effects, magic, illusions | Wisdom |

**Roll d20 + base save + attribute modifier.** Meet or exceed the target number to succeed.

## Death and Dying

- At **0 HP**, a character is unconscious and dying.
- Each round at 0 or below, lose 1 HP.
- At **-10 HP**, the character is dead.
- An ally can stabilize a dying character with a successful check or healing.

## Common Combat Actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Attack** | Strike with a weapon (primary action) |
| **Move** | Move up to your speed |
| **Charge** | Move + attack at +2 to hit, -2 AC until next turn |
| **Withdraw** | Careful retreat without provoking |
| **Parry** | Forgo attack, +2 AC (buckler grants additional +1) |
| **Set for Charge** | Brace a weapon; if charged, deal double damage |
