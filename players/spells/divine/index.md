# Divine Spells

Divine spells are cast by clerics, druids, paladins, and rangers. Unlike arcane spells which draw upon raw magical energy, divine spells are granted through faith and devotion to a deity or through a deep connection to the natural world.

## Spheres of Influence

Divine spellcasters recognize the schools of magic, but also implement spheres of influence that more accurately reflect areas of common worship. A cleric's available spheres are determined by their deity. The spheres are:

- **All** -- Available to all divine casters
- **Animal** -- Spells relating to animals and beasts
- **Charm** -- Spells of influence and persuasion
- **Combat** -- Spells that aid in battle
- **Divination** -- Spells that reveal information
- **Elemental (Water, Fire, Air, Earth)** -- Spells tied to the elements
- **Healing** -- Spells that restore health and vitality
- **Necromantic** -- Spells dealing with life, death, and undeath
- **Plant** -- Spells relating to plant life
- **Protection** -- Spells that ward and defend
- **Sun** -- Spells of light and radiance
- **Weather** -- Spells that control the elements and climate
