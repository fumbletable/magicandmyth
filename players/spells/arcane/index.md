# Arcane Spells

Arcane spells are cast by mages, illusionists, and other practitioners of the arcane arts. These spells draw upon the raw forces of magic to manipulate the world, create illusions, summon creatures, and unleash devastating energies.

- [Arcane Level 1](level-1) -- 45 spells ranging from protective wards to offensive blasts
- [Arcane Level 2](level-2) -- 41 spells of greater complexity and power
- [Arcane Level 3](level-3) -- 34 spells including potent combat magic and advanced illusions
- [Arcane Level 4](level-4) -- 41 spells of formidable power including polymorphs and walls of force
- [Arcane Level 5](level-5) -- 35 spells commanding elemental forces and planar magic
- [Arcane Level 6](level-6) -- 31 spells of devastating might and reality-bending illusions
- [Arcane Level 7](level-7) -- 24 spells of extraordinary power including simulacrum and prismatic spray
- [Arcane Level 8](level-8) -- 20 spells of near-ultimate arcane mastery
- [Arcane Level 9](level-9) -- 17 spells representing the pinnacle of arcane power
