# Arcane Spells

Arcane spells are cast by mages, illusionists, and other practitioners of the arcane arts. These spells draw upon the raw forces of magic to manipulate the world, create illusions, summon creatures, and unleash devastating energies.

- [Arcane Level 1](level-1) -- 37 spells ranging from protective wards to offensive blasts
- [Arcane Level 2](level-2) -- 32 spells of greater complexity and power
- [Arcane Level 3](level-3) -- 27 spells including potent combat magic and advanced illusions
