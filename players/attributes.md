# Attributes

Every character has six attributes that define their physical and mental capabilities. These are rolled during character creation and modified by ancestry.

## Check Modifier

Used for ability checks. Roll d20 + check modifier vs difficulty.

## Constitution (CON)

Affects Fortitude saves and shock survival.
