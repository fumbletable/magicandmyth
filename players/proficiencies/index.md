# Proficiencies

Proficiencies represent your character's trained abilities, both in and out of combat. There are two types:

- [**Weapon Proficiencies**](weapon-proficiencies) — Combat skills including weapons training, fighting styles, and maneuvers training that help a character individualize himself from the standard cloth of his peers.
- [**Nonweapon Proficiencies**](nonweapon-proficiencies) — Skill-based training that relates to interests outside of combat, supporting storytelling and character development.
