# Proficiencies

Proficiencies represent your character's trained skills and weapon expertise. There are two types:

- [**Weapon Proficiencies**](weapon-proficiencies) — Which weapons you can use effectively
- [**Nonweapon Proficiencies**](nonweapon-proficiencies) — Skills, trades, and knowledge
