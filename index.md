# Magic&Myth

Welcome to the digital companion for **Magic&Myth**, a tabletop roleplaying game by Scott Myers.

This site is a complete reference for players and referees — ancestries, classes, equipment, spells, combat rules, and more. Everything you need at the table, searchable and always up to date.
